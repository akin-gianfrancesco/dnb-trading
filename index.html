D & B

D & B General Trading Co are Australian based consultants and brokers leading in global trading, logistics, business strategy, advisory and project management.

D & B General Trading Co operate in the PPE trading seqta, as international brokers. The main PPE products are CE / FDA approved are nitrile gloves & micro touch gloves.

Kimerly-Clark Nitrile Gloves
CE / FDA Approved PPE

Kimberly-Clark Purple Nitrile Exam Gloves provide worry-free protection. These purple nitrile gloves are powder-free and do not contain natural rubber latex (some say "latex-free"). They are composed of a highly durable nitrile polymer. The gloves are 9.5 inches long, ambidextrous, and include a beaded cuff for easy on and off. Because of the textured fingertips, your employees will have outstanding dexterity and tactile sensitivity. They've even been given the go-ahead to be used in chemotherapy.

Ansell Edge 82-133 Nitrile Gloves
CE / FDA Approved PPE

Ansell gloves provide the highest hand protection while also sustaining the glove durability. Ansell's continued efforts to develop the next generation of nitrile gloves have resulted in gloves with unrivalled flexibility and tensile strength, outperforming even some of the thicker nitrile gloves on the market. For medical examinations and many other activities that require PPE protection, Ansell 82-133 Nitrile gloves guarantee the ultimate confidence and reliability.

Ansell Micro Touch Gloves
CE / FDA Approved PPE

A poylacrylate inner coating is designed for this non-sterile glove, for easy donning and removal making it perfect for users who need to change gloves frequently. Ansell's natural rubber latex formulation produces a pleasant, well-fitting glove with a very low residual protein content thanks to extensive leaching processes. It is certified and CE Marked as Category III Personal Protective Equipment, as well as CE Marked as a Class I Medical Device ensuring more consistent quality and offering protection for a wide range of applications.
